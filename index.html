The Wedding of Ashley Desjardins & John Hall

Thank you for visiting our website! We hope you will find everything you're looking for and we are so excited for you to be a part of our big day!

September 10th, 2016

We're hoping for a clear-skied evening of fun and dancing! Typically, temperatures in early September here in Vermont are mild and comfortable in the early evening. Not too hot, not too cold, perfect for gettin' your groove on!

Greensboro, Vermont

The festivities will be held at 28 Edsall Road, Greensboro VT 05841. The ceremony will take place in a large field with a beautiful view of Caspian Lake. Dinner and dancing will be held in a large tent on the property, just a short walk from the Ceremony site.
